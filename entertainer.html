About the Act

Music, Performance & Pure Energy

With over two decades of karaoke show hosting and wedding DJ'ing experience, John brings a unique blend of crowd-reading DJ skills and the rare ability to turn a quiet room into an unforgettable party. Whether it's a romantic first dance at a wedding reception, a rowdy bar karaoke night, or an original set at a summer festival — John delivers every time.

As a karaoke KJ, John specializes in creating inclusive shows where every singer feels like a star. His wedding DJ services feature MC skills and custom playlists built around the couple's vision.
