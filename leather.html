About Simpson Leather Craft

Crafted by Hand. Built to Last.

Simpson Leather Craft is a passion-driven leather goods business founded on a love for traditional craftsmanship and quality materials. Every piece is cut, stitched, and finished by hand — no shortcuts, no mass production.

From everyday carry items like wallets and key fobs to fully custom holsters, belts, and personalized gifts, each product is designed to develop a rich patina over time and become a cherished, one-of-a-kind piece.

Whether you're looking for something off the shelf from the Etsy shop or a fully custom commission, the same care and attention to detail goes into every single item.
