QA Mindset

Quality Assurance is not just about finding bugs — it's about building quality in from the start. My QA approach is rooted in deep attention to detail, systematic thinking, and a genuine commitment to delivering software that works reliably for real users.

My background in operating, monitoring, and troubleshooting complex automated systems (SWISSLOG cranes, CMMS platforms, telecom equipment) has given me a strong foundation in real-world system behavior, failure modes, and edge case identification. I understand what breaks in production, and I test for it.

Combined with my software development skills, I bring a developer-tester perspective that helps bridge the gap between teams and catch issues earlier in the development lifecycle.

My QA Process

01

02

Plan Test Coverage

03
